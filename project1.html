Q.A.Muham Ali jinah

1931-2015

Hero Man of pakistan

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnahborn on December 25, 1876, in Karachi.played a pivotal role in the creation of Pakistan and is revered as the father of the nation. A barristepolitician, and statesman, Jinnah served as the leader of the All-India Muslim League from 1913 until Pakistan's independence in 1947. His tireless efforts culminated in the establishment of Pakistan as a separate nation for Muslim Jinnah's achievements as the founder of Pakistan overshadowed his other endeavors, reflecting his dedication to the cause of independence[3]. Unfortunately, he faced health challenges in his later years and passed away on September 11, 1948, in Karachi. His legacy continues to shape the identity of Pakistan, and he is remembered for his vision, leadership, and unwavering commitment to the ideals of the nation.
